What the platform is meant to do

Loanfy is a practical workflow concept for finance teams that need better order around borrower intake, documents, internal stages and handoff. It is not presented as an instant approval engine or a replacement for underwriting. It is a way to make the work before and around underwriting more consistent.

The platform view starts with the borrower or broker submission. The request should capture product type, borrower details, use of funds, collateral or business context, documents and timing. From there, the internal team needs status, ownership and missing-item visibility. If the file moves forward, the same context should remain available for decision, closing or servicing.

Teams can adopt the platform thinking in stages. A borrower portal might be the first improvement. A document automation workflow might be next. Later, CRM views and reporting can connect the pieces. That staged approach keeps the project grounded in actual operational pain.
