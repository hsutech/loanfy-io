The blog exists to do two jobs at once. First, it gives the site depth and legitimacy. Second, it catches visitors who are not ready to fill out a form yet but are actively trying to understand their borrowing options. Every article here includes a table of contents, author line, featured visual, internal links and enough depth to feel genuinely useful. The layout stays editorial rather than boxed-in so the pages feel like reading experiences, not decorative cards with copy squeezed into them.
