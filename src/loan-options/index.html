Routing borrowers into the most relevant category.

Not every visitor should land on the same kind of page. Someone trying to simplify expensive balances has a different problem from someone covering an urgent repair or planning a renovation. The purpose of this section is to route people into the most relevant category before they submit a request.

Every page explains who the category is for, what expenses it usually covers, how the process works, what information strengthens the request and which related options may deserve a look first. If you are not sure where to start, begin with the category that best matches the main reason you need funds right now.
