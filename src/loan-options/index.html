Borrower summary

Requested amount, purpose, timeline and the details that help shape a more useful follow-up.

Not every visitor should land on the same kind of page. Someone trying to simplify expensive balances has a different problem from someone covering an urgent repair or planning a renovation. The purpose of this section is to route people into the most relevant category before they submit a request. That improves trust, lowers confusion and tends to create stronger lead quality.

Every page in this section explains who the category is for, what expenses it usually covers, how the process works, what information strengthens the request and which related options may deserve a look first. That structure is more useful than a generic category grid because it actively helps the visitor choose.

If you are not sure where to start, begin with the category that best matches the main reason you need funds right now. Then compare it with at least one neighboring option so the choice is intentional rather than accidental.
